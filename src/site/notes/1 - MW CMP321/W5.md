**INTERPRETATION:** `<S> → X|Y|X <S> | Y <S>` 
> [!success]- What is the interpretation?
> - **INTERPRETATION:** List of one or more X's or Y's (non-empty)

**CONSTRUCTION**: Write a grammar that will produce (1 rule or two): Lists of X's and/or Y's that start with X and end with Y
> [!success]- Solution
> `<S> → XY | X <XY> Y`
> `<XY> → X | Y | X <XY> | Y <XY>`
